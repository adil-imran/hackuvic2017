About Clifford D. May

Clifford D. May is president of the Foundation for Defense of Democracies (FDD) and a columnist for the Washington Times. He was this month appointed to the US Commission on International Religious Freedom (USCIRF), an independent bipartisan federal body reporting to the President, Secretary of State, and Congress. Opinions expressed in this column are solely his own.
