Baku, April 21 AZERTAC

The Egyptian newspaper has published an article by employee of the Oriental Studies Institute of the Azerbaijan National Academy of Sciences (ANAS) Mahir Hamidov on Armenian provocative acts.

The article highlights the recent developments in the line of the contacts of Armenian and Azerbaijani troops, as well as Armenian massacre and ethnic cleansing policy perpetrated against Azerbaijan over the centuries.
