Peshawar: Heavy firing is taking place inside the Bacha Khan University in Charsadda town in Pakistan. While two terrorists have been killed, eight to ten terrorists are reportedly inside the campus.

According to reports, over 3000 students along with additional 600 guests are present at the university for a scheduled event.

Students are being evacuated from classes and are being moved to safer locations.

Security forces have entered the area and combing operation is underway. Heavy contingents of elite force have been deployed in and around the area.

Taliban terrorists had massacred over 150 people, mostly students, in an attack on an army-run school in Peshawar on December 16, 2014.
