David Beirne, an Irish hospital executive, has been named vice president of UPMC International and country manager for Ireland.

Beirne previously served as chief executive officer at the Whitfield Clinic, the private hospital in Waterford that has been home to the UPMC Whitfield Cancer Centre for the last decade. Beirne will be responsible for overseeing the development of UPMC's new joint venture cancer center in Cork, announced last week in partnership with Bon Secours Health System.
