10 ISIL Militants Killed During Battles in Iraq's Anbar Province

TEHRAN (FNA)- Iraqi security forces and local tribal fighters killed at least 10 ISIL militants during an operation in Western Anbar province, media reported.

During the operation, the Iraqi pro-government forces engaged in hours-long clashes in the province of Anbar which also ended in two Iraqi security officers and two tribal leaders injured, Sputnik reported.

The Western Iraqi province of Anbar has been mired in clashes this week, according to the media reports. .

ISIL, a terrorist group outlawed by UN and many other countries, has seized territories in Syria and Iraq since 2014.
