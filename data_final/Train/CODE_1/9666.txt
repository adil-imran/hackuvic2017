PanARMENIAN.Net - The operative situation on the Nagorno Karabakh-Azerbaijan line of contact remained unchanged on the night of May 9-10.

As Karabakh Defense Army reports, the rival used various caliber firearms, 60- and 82-mm mortars to violate the verbal agreement on the ceasefire.

The Nagorno Karabakh frontline units continue protecting their positions, taking retaliatory measure if necessary.
